About Us

We are Journify.

From November 2019, our company has been providing quality affordable products which helped thousands of costumers around the US and Canada.

Our main service is the online journaling web application, which allows users who have an account to write stories and publish them in our Journify Blog.

We understand how important journaling can be in someone's life. So, to reach everyone with any social-economical status, we offer products such as notebooks, pens, pencils and markers for a very affordable price. (A notebook comes with a free pencil !!!)

- Arsen Hakobyan, Founder of Journify.
